## 🏛️ THE PIECES (SIX OF THEM. I COUNTED.)

### 👑 IMPERATOR
basically the king. moves 1 square. any direction. hes SLOW. hes OLD. hes IMPORTANT.

if this guy dies ur done. game over. pack it up. go home.

BUT WAIT THERES MORE 🎉🎉🎉

**IMPERATOR COMMAND™** - ok ok ok so this is the cool part. once per turn the imperator can SCREAM at a nearby friendly piece and they get an EXTRA MOVE. like a free bonus move. just for existing near him. is this balanced? absolutely not. do i care? look at my face. do i look like i care?

(he cant command while in check tho bc hes too busy having a panic attack)

### 🏇 LEGATE
its a rook. i named it something cooler so it sounds like i put effort in. moves in straight lines. up down left right. BOOOORING but reliable. hes that one friend who always shows up on time

### ⚔️ TRIBUNE
bishop but make it ✨roman✨. diagonal moves only. skrrrrt across the board. honestly hes just vibes

### 🐴 EQUES
THE HORSE GUY. moves in an L. you know. 2 and 1. like the letter L. except its a horse. horses dont move in L shapes irl but this one does. respect the L. he JUMPS over pieces because gravity is a suggestion and horses dont care about ur pawns blocking the way

### 🚩 SIGNIFER (MY FAVORITE I LOVE HIM)
OK SO. this guy. THIS GUY RIGHT HERE. hes the reason i made this game.

he has TWO options on his turn:
1. move 1 square in a cardinal direction (up down left right, you know the ones)
2. OR just. delete. an adjacent enemy. without moving. just YEET them off the board

THATS RIGHT. he can just remove a guy from existence. all 8 directions. left right up down AND the diagonals. hes surrounded on all sides by potential victims and honestly? same.

is this overpowered? maybe. is it hilarious? ABSOLUTELY. would i change it? never in a million years

### ⚔️ MILITES
pawn but i used google translate and found the latin word for soldier so now its fancy

moves DIAGNALLY FORWARD ONLY. no straight moves. no sideways. no backwards. diagonal forward or NOTHING.

BUT WHEN HE REACHES THE OTHER SIDE he PROMOTES and becomes literally ANY PIECE HE WANTS. imperator? sure. another signifer? why not. FIVE IMPERATORS?? the game literally allows it and i think thats beautiful chaos

## ⚠️ RULES (i know rules are boring but SIT DOWN)

### 🔴 Check
when ur imperator is in danger. ur in check. u HAVE to fix it. u cant just pretend its not happening like i do with my homework. move him. block it. kill the threat. SOMETHING. or u lose.

### 💀 Stalemate
in normal chess this is a draw. in MY chess? no. if u have no legal moves u just LOSE lol. this isnt a bug. this is because i didnt want to code draw detection and now its a FEATURE. repeat after me: its not a bug its a feature 🗿

### 📢 Imperator Command (again bc its important)
- ONCE per TURN not per piece. turn. T U R N.
- yellow glow = "hey this guy can be yelled at"
- click him = free bonus move for that piece
- dont click = nothing happens no pressure
- resets every turn automatically i aint making u remember stuff

### ⬆️ Promotion
milites touches the other side → popup shows up → click what u want → boom ur piece now. thats the whole system. its not complicated i promise
